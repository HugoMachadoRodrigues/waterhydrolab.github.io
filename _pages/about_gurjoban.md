**Gurjoban Singh Tiwana**
Graduate Research Assistant · M.S. Student
*Joined January 2025*

Gurjoban is from India and earned his bachelor's in Agricultural
Engineering from Punjab Agricultural University, where his core work
focused on watershed hydrology, soil dynamics, and precision
agriculture. In his M.S. program he investigates the effects of nitrogen
fertilization and irrigation on silage-corn yield and nitrate leaching
in sandy Spodosols, integrating sensor-based monitoring to advance
sustainable nutrient management and support environmentally responsible
farming.
