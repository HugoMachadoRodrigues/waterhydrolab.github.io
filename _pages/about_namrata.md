**Namrata Ghimire**
Biological Scientist II · Ph.D. Student
*Joined May 2025*

Originally from Nepal, Namrata completed her undergraduate studies at
the Agriculture and Forestry University, Nepal, and earned her M.S. from
South Dakota State University (Fall 2023), where her research focused on
soil health under different cover crops and grazing integration. She
joined UF-RCREC as a Biological Scientist II in August 2023 and began
working with Dr. Golmohammadi in May 2025. Her PhD research applies AI
and physically based models to water management and water-quality
problems.
